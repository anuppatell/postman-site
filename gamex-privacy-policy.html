Your Game Files

GameX - PS2 Emulator allows you to import your own PS2 game files (ISO, BIN, CHD, CSO, ISZ, ELF) from your device's local storage.

✓  Your game files are processed entirely on-device. They are never uploaded to our servers, shared with third parties, or transmitted over the internet in any form.

Save states, game progress, and any data generated during gameplay remain stored locally on your device and are solely under your control.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all data collection by simply uninstalling GameX - PS2 Emulator from your device. Use the standard uninstall process available on your iOS device or via the App Store.

Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored app data, including your imported game library and any saved preferences.
